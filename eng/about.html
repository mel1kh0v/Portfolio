About me

My name is Kirill, and I work as a product designer. I have over a 2 years of real-world experience in the industry.

I have a deep passion for design, and I strongly believe in its ability to revolutionize the way people interact with technology. My design approach is centered around the user, as I prioritize understanding their needs and creating designs that are intuitive, visually appealing, and functional, addressing any challenges they may face.

I also dabble in drawing, and I have a great appreciation for movies and orchestral music. I find it important to stay inspired and curious about the world around me, as it helps me bring fresh perspectives and ideas to my design work.

Currently, I am working as a product designer on a freelance, striving to develop innovative and user-friendly products. I'm always open to new opportunities and would be delighted to hear from you if you have a project in mind that you'd like to collaborate on.

Feel free to reach out to me at melikhov.design@gmail.com to discuss your project or simply say hello!
